Week 15: Website Strategy & Purpose

This week focuses on **planning your website’s purpose, audience, and structure**, helping you create a clear strategy before building anything.

Day 1: Define Your Purpose

Learn: Every successful website starts with a purpose. Think about what problem your site solves, who it serves, and what makes it unique. Do: Write down 1–3 sentences describing your website’s main goal. Check: You should have a clear, actionable purpose statement that guides all future design and content decisions.

Day 2: Audience Research

Learn: Understand who your visitors are. Consider age, interests, tech comfort, and goals. Do: Create a small profile of 2–3 target audience types and what they would expect from your site. Check: Profiles should inform decisions like content type, layout, and design style.

Day 3: Planning Site Structure

Learn: Organize your pages logically. Sketch a sitemap with main pages (Home, About, Contact, Blog) and subpages if needed. Do: Draw a diagram on paper or a tool showing page hierarchy and navigation flow. Check: You should see clear paths between pages, showing how users move through your site.

Day 4: Branding Basics

Learn: Consistent branding helps users recognize your site. Pick a color palette, font styles, and a logo style. Do: Write down 2–3 main colors, 1–2 fonts, and sketch a simple logo idea. Check: Choices should reflect your site’s personality and be consistent across all pages.

Day 5: Content Strategy

Learn: Plan what content you’ll post, how often, and in what format (text, images, video). Do: Make a table or list of content topics for the next month and decide which pages they belong to. Check: You should have a clear plan showing what content goes where and how it helps your audience.

Day 6: Goal Setting

Learn: Define measurable goals to track success, like signups, clicks, or newsletter subscriptions. Do: Write down 2–3 goals with metrics (e.g., “50 newsletter signups in first month”). Check: Goals should be realistic, measurable, and aligned with your site purpose.

Day 7: Weekly Review

Learn: Review your purpose, audience, site structure, branding, content, and goals. Do: Refine any sections that feel unclear or incomplete. Check: At the end of the week, you should have a **comprehensive site strategy document** to guide design and development in future weeks.
