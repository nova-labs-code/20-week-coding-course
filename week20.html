Week 20: Launch, Maintenance & Growth

This final week focuses on **launching your website professionally, maintaining it, and planning future growth** for long-term success.

Day 1: Final Site Check

Learn: Review every page, link, form, and interactive element. Do: Test all pages in browser, check forms, and verify images. Check: All elements should work correctly without errors.

Day 2: Backups & Security

Learn: Protect your site with SSL, regular backups, and strong passwords. Do: Enable SSL, backup your site files, and set secure passwords. Check: Backup works and SSL is active.

Day 3: Hosting & Domain

Learn: Ensure your hosting provider is reliable, and your domain is ready. Do: Verify hosting speed, uptime, and domain setup. Check: Site is accessible online at your chosen domain.

Day 4: Launch Checklist

Learn: Prepare for launch: SEO, analytics, social links, email setup, and announcement plan. Do: Follow a checklist to make sure nothing is missing. Check: All elements are ready and functional for public launch.

Day 5: Post-Launch Monitoring

Learn: Monitor traffic, errors, and user feedback. Do: Check analytics and fix any issues reported. Check: Errors are resolved quickly, and users can navigate smoothly.

Day 6: Continuous Improvement

Learn: Use analytics and feedback to iterate and improve. Do: Pick one page and implement an improvement based on data. Check: Improvement results in better user engagement or performance.
