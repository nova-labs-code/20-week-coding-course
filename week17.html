Week 17: Content & SEO Basics – Extended

This week focuses on **optimizing content and search engine strategies** while building pages that are readable, fast, and discoverable. Exercises and tips help make content actionable.

Day 1: Keyword Research

Learn: Keywords are what people type into search engines. Use tools like Google Keyword Planner, Ubersuggest, or brainstorm related terms. Focus on relevance and search volume.

Do: Make a list of 15–20 keywords related to your site topic. Include long-tail keywords (3–5 words) that are highly relevant.

Mini Exercise: Write down 5 keywords you would target for your homepage and 5 for your blog posts.
Tip: Use natural language; avoid stuffing keywords unnaturally.
Day 2: On-Page SEO

Learn: Optimize each page for search engines:

  • <title> – clear, includes keyword
  • Headings (<h1>, <h2>) – structure content and include keywords
  • Image alt text – describe image for accessibility and SEO
  • URLs – readable, short, include keyword

Do: Pick a page and rewrite its title, headings, and image alt text using your keywords.

Mini Exercise: Compare your old page vs optimized page; check if headings flow naturally and include target keywords.
Tip: Keywords should appear naturally; search engines penalize overuse.
Day 3: Internal Linking

Learn: Connect related pages using links. Improves user navigation and SEO. Examples: "Read more about branding → About Page".

Do: Add internal links between your pages, especially blog posts, service pages, or tutorials.

Mini Exercise: Identify 3–5 pages and link them logically. Test all links are working and labeled clearly.
Tip: Use descriptive anchor text; avoid “click here”.
Day 4: Content Formatting

Learn: Readable pages keep users engaged. Use:

  • Headings for hierarchy
  • Bulleted or numbered lists for clarity
  • Bold or italics for emphasis
  • Images or videos to illustrate concepts
  • Short paragraphs

Do: Take a page and reformat it using headings, lists, and visuals.

Mini Exercise: Have a friend read the page and tell the main points; adjust if unclear.
Tip: Skimmers should understand key points without reading everything.
Day 5: Performance Optimization

Learn: Page speed affects SEO and user experience. Steps to optimize:

  • Compress images
  • Minimize JS & CSS files
  • Remove unnecessary scripts or plugins
  • Use caching and CDN if possible

Do: Optimize images on a page, remove unused code, and test load time.

Mini Exercise: Use a tool like Google PageSpeed Insights and document current vs optimized speed score.
Tip: Faster pages reduce bounce rate and improve rankings.
Day 6: Analytics Setup

Learn: Tracking user behavior helps improve content and SEO. Use Google Analytics or similar tools. Track pageviews, user flow, and conversions.

Do: Add tracking code to your site and verify it records visits.

Mini Exercise: Visit pages and confirm real-time traffic appears in analytics. Note most visited pages.
Tip: Data-driven adjustments outperform guessing.
Day 7: Review & Adjust

Learn: Combine all SEO and content lessons:

  • Check keywords in titles, headings, alt text
  • Confirm internal links work
  • Verify readability and formatting
  • Ensure page speed is optimized
  • Confirm analytics tracking

Do: Create a checklist, review your pages, and make adjustments.

Mini Exercise: Pick 3 pages and fully optimize them for SEO and readability. Test links, images, and speed.
Tip: Optimization is ongoing; review periodically after adding new content.
