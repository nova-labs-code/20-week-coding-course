Week 5: CSS Styling & Effects

This week we focus on **advanced CSS styling** to make your pages visually appealing with shadows, borders, rounded corners, and gradients. Follow “Learn → See → Do → Check” to practice daily.

Day 1: Rounded Corners & Borders

Learn: CSS allows you to style elements with border-radius for rounded corners and customize borders with width, style, and color. See:

div {border: 2px solid #333; border-radius: 10px; padding: 10px;}
Do: Add a rounded border to one of your sections or boxes on your profile page. Check: Open the browser to ensure corners are rounded and borders display correctly.

Day 2: Box Shadows

Learn: Shadows create depth using box-shadow, e.g., box-shadow: 5px 5px 10px rgba(0,0,0,0.3);. Do: Add a shadow to your header or cards to make them pop. Check: Open in a browser to see the shadow effect and adjust offsets and blur to experiment with the appearance.

Day 3: Text Shadows & Typography Effects

Learn: Text shadows enhance headings with text-shadow and CSS controls font-weight, style, and spacing. See:

h1 {text-shadow: 2px 2px 5px gray;}
p {letter-spacing: 1px; line-height:1.5;}
Do: Apply a subtle shadow to your headings and adjust paragraph letter-spacing for readability. Check: Open in a browser and see how typography changes the visual hierarchy.

Day 4: Gradients

Learn: CSS gradients create smooth color transitions. Linear gradients go from top to bottom or left to right. See:

div {background: linear-gradient(to right, #ff7e5f, #feb47b);}
Do: Add a gradient background to a section or box. Check: Open the browser to see the gradient and try changing directions or colors.

Day 5: Hover Effects

Learn: Use :hover pseudo-class for interactive effects. See:

a:hover {color: red; text-decoration: underline;}
div:hover {background-color: #eee;}
Do: Add hover effects to links and sections on your page. Check: Move your mouse over the elements to verify effects.

Day 6: Combining Effects

Learn: You can combine multiple styles: borders, shadows, gradients, and hover effects together. See:

div.card {
  border-radius: 10px; 
  box-shadow: 2px 2px 10px rgba(0,0,0,0.2);
  background: linear-gradient(to right, #a1c4fd, #c2e9fb);
}
div.card:hover {transform: scale(1.05);}
Do: Apply combined styles to a box or card on your page. Check: Observe how hover and shadows improve visual appeal.

Day 7: Review & Styled Mini Page

Learn: Combine all Week 5 styling techniques: rounded corners, shadows, gradients, text shadows, and hover effects. Do: Enhance your profile page with at least one section using these effects. Check: Open in a browser to ensure styles display correctly and that hover effects and shadows improve the visual design.
